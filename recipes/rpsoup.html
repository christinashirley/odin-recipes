Red Pepper Soup

Description

Something about my childhood smelling like sauteed garlic and simmering tomatoes.

Feel free to replace all of the ingredients with literally anything else, and let me know how much better it tastes!

Ingredients

  • 2 16-oz jars roasted red peppers
  • 2 14.5oz cans chicken broth
  • G1 8oz package sliced fresh tomatoes
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 9-oz packages fresh cheese tortellini, uncooked

Steps

  1. Place roasted red peppers in a blender or food processor, and blend until smooth.
  2. In a large saucepan, mix roasted red peppers, chicken broth, and mushrooms. Season with basil, oregano, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Bring the mixture to a boil.
  3. Stir in tortellini, and continue cooking 8 to 10 minutes, or until pasta is al dente.
